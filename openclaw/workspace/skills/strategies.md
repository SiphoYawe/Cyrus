# Strategies Skill

## Trigger Phrases
- "what strategies are running"
- "strategy performance"
- "show strategies"
- "which strategies are active"
- "strategy P&L"

## Workflow

1. Call `strategies` tool (with optional name filter)
2. Show combined P&L across all strategies
3. List each strategy with tier, win rate, and P&L
4. Highlight best and worst performers
5. Show open positions per strategy
