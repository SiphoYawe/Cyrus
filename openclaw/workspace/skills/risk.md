# Risk Skill

## Trigger Phrases
- "what's my risk level"
- "set risk to X"
- "risk dial"
- "change risk"
- "conservative mode"
- "aggressive mode"

## Workflow

### View Risk
1. Call `risk-dial` tool without parameters
2. Show current dial level with visual indicator
3. Show tier allocation breakdown

### Set Risk
1. Map natural language to dial level if needed
2. Call `risk-dial` tool with level parameter
3. Show old vs new allocation comparison
4. Explain impact of the change

## Risk Level Guide

| Level | Style | Safe | Growth | Degen |
|-------|-------|------|--------|-------|
| 1-2 | Ultra Conservative | 80-90% | 5-15% | 0-4% |
| 3-4 | Conservative | 60-80% | 15-25% | 4-10% |
| 5 | Balanced | 50% | 30% | 15% |
| 6-7 | Growth | 30-50% | 30-35% | 15-30% |
| 8-10 | Aggressive | 10-30% | 35-40% | 30-45% |
